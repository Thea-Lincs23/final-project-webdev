About us

3M pac tires was founded by Henry Paculaba an electrician back then and was inspired of bulding a tire shop business of his own, until one day after his hardwork of being an electrician he built his own business called 3m pac tires. The shop was named in honour of his son and two daughters named Michael,Mary ann and Mikaela. 3M pac tires was established in MJ Cuenco Avenue Cebu City and it is known the best selling dealership of brand new surplus tires in town. 3M pac tires continue to grow and adapt to the demands of the market to provide the experience that you deserve.
